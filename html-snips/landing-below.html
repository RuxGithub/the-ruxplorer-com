Who's Rux?

Rux is an identity.
It was created to express the sides of a person that they couldnt express otherwise
It was named after a resemblance to a name and the resemblance of it's state of mind to the meaning of rux.
Being driven by the curiosity and wonder about why things are, how they work and how they are made, Rux came to explore them in a vast range of topics that are believed to not work well together.
With no real outlet or reason to apply what it found on the way, Rux started working on them regardless of the reason other than the desire to do so.
Between the complexity of not really existing and the existing constraint of time, it started chipping away into the depths of it's curiosity and bit by bit started to produce.
What it's producing, it will share with you here.
Feel free to explore alongside it.
